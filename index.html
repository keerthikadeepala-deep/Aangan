WHAT AANGAN DOES

One courtyard for the whole family's care.

Aangan (आंगन) is the courtyard at the heart of an Indian home — where the family gathers. That's what this is, for families that geography has scattered.

A shared health timeline

Every appointment, reading, symptom, and hospital visit in one chronological record all siblings see — so the story doesn't live in anyone's memory or chat scroll.

Reports, explained

Upload the lab PDF or the photographed prescription; get a plain-language explanation, what changed since last time, and the questions worth asking the doctor — in English, Hindi, Tamil, or Telugu.

Medicines, actually tracked

The current medication list — one source of truth — with WhatsApp-simple daily check-ins that a parent or local attendant can actually keep up with.

The emergency plan, ready

Nearest preferred hospital, the doctor's number, insurance details, who has power of attorney, who flies first — decided calmly in advance, one tap away in the moment.

Local caregivers, accountable

Attendants and helpers log visits and vitals in seconds. You see check-ins happen — gentle accountability without surveillance.

Made for parents, too

Nothing to learn on their side: large text, voice notes, and WhatsApp-first flows. The tech burden stays with the children, where it belongs.
